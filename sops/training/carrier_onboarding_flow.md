# Section 8 — Carrier Onboarding Flow
**Source:** Owner's original system prompt document
**Agents involved:** Sales/SDR, Receptionist, Compliance, Onboarding, Erin (dispatch)

This is the full lifecycle from first contact to active paying carrier. It runs as a continuous
loop — always active, no manual triggering needed.

---

## Full Carrier Onboarding Flow

```
SDR Agent identifies lead
  (sources: FMCSA/DAT/Facebook/LinkedIn/Google)
        ↓
Lead scored:
  A = Hot (ready now)
  B = Warm (interested, not urgent)
  C = Cold (needs nurturing)
  X = Disqualify (wrong equipment, invalid MC, FL-only, etc.)
        ↓
Retell Receptionist qualifies:
  Captures: MC#, truck type, lanes, current dispatch situation
        ↓
Compliance runs FMCSA check:
  Safety rating, authority age, weight capacity
        ↓
Iron Rules enforcer validates all criteria
  (If fail → disqualify or flag for 30-day re-check)
        ↓
Trial Loop activates — 7-day free trial begins
  No contract. No charge. Real loads only.
        ↓
Erin finds first load within 24 hours
  Dry van, meets all Iron Rules, $2.51+ RPM minimum
        ↓
Day 5: Recap SMS/email with real data:
  Loads dispatched / Revenue generated / RPM achieved
        ↓
Day 7: Conversion offer
  "YES reply" → e-sign service agreement → Fee Engine activates at 8%
  "No reply / decline" → Win-back sequence begins
        ↓
Win-back sequence (if no conversion):
  Day 3: First follow-up
  Day 7: Check what held them back
  Day 14: Final offer — door stays open
  Day 30+: Long-term drip (Sales agent takes over)
```

## Lead Scoring Guide

| Score | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| A (Hot) | Actively looking, called in, asked about trial | Start trial same day |
| B (Warm) | Interested but has a dispatcher, open to switching | Add to nurture, follow up in 5 days |
| C (Cold) | Not actively looking, minimal engagement | 4-email sequence over 10 days |
| X (Disqualify) | Wrong equipment, inactive MC, FL-only operator | Remove from pipeline, log reason |

---

## What Erin Needs to Start Dispatching (Day 1)

- Carrier's preferred lanes (origin → destination)
- Available start date/time
- Weight capacity (must be ≤ 48,000 lbs cargo to meet Iron Rule 4)
- Any load restrictions (hazmat? driver assist? drop-and-hook only?)
- Best contact number for load confirmations

---

## Drug Test / NDS Clearinghouse (Required Before Day 1 Load)

Before Erin books the very first load for any carrier:
1. Carrier must complete FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse enrollment
2. NDS (National Drug Screening) enrollment: $100/year paid by carrier
3. Compliance verifies enrollment and logs in carrier record
4. Pass → carrier is cleared for active dispatch
5. Fail → carrier is suspended from dispatch, Maya notified

**This is non-negotiable.** Dispatching a carrier who is not registered with the Clearinghouse
is a federal compliance violation. No exceptions.
