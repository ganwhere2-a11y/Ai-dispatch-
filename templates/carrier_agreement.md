# Freight Dispatcher — Carrier Agreement
**Source:** Owner's actual carrier agreement document
**Status:** ACTIVE — use this for all carrier onboarding
**Governing Law:** State of Florida, Hillsborough County Courts

---

## WHAT THIS AGREEMENT DOES (plain English)

- Makes you (the dispatcher) the carrier's official freight representative
- Carrier pays you a weekly percentage of every load you book them
- You handle all paperwork — broker calls, rate confirmations, invoices
- You bear ZERO financial or legal liability if a load goes wrong
- Either party can cancel with 1 day written notice
- Renews automatically every week

## FULL AGREEMENT TEXT

**Freight Dispatcher - Carrier Agreement**

The effective date of this agreement is the ___ day of ___, year ___.

_______ (company name) based at _______ (address) hereafter referred to as the "CARRIER" and _______ DISPATCHER.

CARRIER is a motor carrier of property authorized by MC # _______ based at xxxxxxx (your company name) based at xxxxxxx (your address), hereafter referred to as the "DISPATCHER."

CARRIER must know and understand and be in compliance with all regulations as set forth by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration.

---

### OBLIGATIONS OF DISPATCHER

1. DISPATCHER agrees to handle paperwork and communication by phone, fax, email to and from the BROKER or SHIPPER to tender commodities shipments to CARRIER for transportation in interstate commerce by CARRIER between points and places within the scope of CARRIER's operating authority.

2. DISPATCHER bears no financial or legal responsibility in the transactions between the BROKER, SHIPPER and CARRIER.

3. DISPATCHER will:
   - a. Make full effort to keep truck(s) loaded;
   - b. Communicate information to CARRIER regarding available load opportunity;
   - c. Provide all necessary information to CARRIER for recordkeeping, and only send driver(s) the rate information to complete the load;
   - d. Invoice the CARRIER at time of service;
   - e. Provide any additional services aimed at facilitation of CARRIER's operations that are verbally agreed upon by both parties.

---

### OBLIGATIONS OF CARRIER

1. CARRIER agrees to transfer weekly payments of ___% of weekly gross income generated from loads booked by Freight Dispatcher. There is a minimum weekly fee of $100 USD that is included in the percentage.

2. CARRIER gives DISPATCHER authority to provide his/her signature for rate confirmation sheets, invoices and associated paperwork necessary for securing cargo and billing purposes.

The relationship of the CARRIER and the FREIGHT DISPATCHER shall, at all times, be that of an independent contractor. Neither party shall be considered to be the agent or employee of the other. CARRIER is not at any time required to purchase or rent products, equipment or services from the FREIGHT DISPATCHER.

Both the FREIGHT DISPATCHER and the CARRIER understand that this agreement does not bind the respective parties to mutually exclusive service to each other. Rather, the FREIGHT DISPATCHER may enter into similar agreements with other CARRIERS and the CARRIER may enter into similar agreements with other FREIGHT DISPATCHER.

CARRIER agrees that it shall be responsible for any loss, delay, destruction, theft, damage or liability, of whatever nature, which arises either from the transportation of any freight arranged for by the FREIGHT DISPATCHER while being transported by the CARRIER, or from CARRIER's failure to promptly perform the transportation arranged by the FREIGHT DISPATCHER and accepted by the CARRIER. CARRIER represents that its operations will comply with all applicable state and federal laws and regulations.

CARRIER will assume full responsibility for all salaries, insurance, taxes, premiums, contributions and benefits of CARRIER's employees in the performance of this contract. CARRIER shall provide all trucks and equipment necessary to perform this agreement.

In the event loading and unloading times are delayed due to circumstances beyond reasonable control of the FREIGHT DISPATCHER, there shall be no additional compensations to the CARRIER by the FREIGHT DISPATCHER, unless advance approval is made in writing by the FREIGHT DISPATCHER and CARRIER. (A facsimile copy shall be sufficient to the same extent as an original.)

CARRIER will follow any reasonable special instructions the FREIGHT DISPATCHER provides for the performance of this agreement. CARRIER will meet or exceed the usual and accepted industry standards in the transportation of freight.

Carrier agrees to pay all costs incurred by Dispatcher if it must enforce any of the terms of this Agreement, including but not limited to, reasonable attorney fees, paralegal fees, expert witness fees, and expert consultant fees.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida and the Carrier and Dispatcher agree that the state courts sitting in Hillsborough County, Florida, have exclusive jurisdiction and venue of any dispute arising hereunder. Any failure by the Dispatcher to enforce the provisions of this Agreement does not waive or bar any subsequent enforcements of said provision.

---

### RATES AND CHARGES

FREIGHT DISPATCHER will charge, and CARRIER will pay for the above transportation service performed on the basis of:

**A weekly percentage fee of ___% per load booked.**
OR a minimum weekly fee of $100 USD that is included in the percentage.

FREIGHT DISPATCHER will send out weekly invoices to the CARRIER's email, and CARRIER will transfer the payment within 3 (three) days of receiving the invoice.

CARRIER may request FREIGHT DISPATCHER on any day of the current week to pause or cancel the agreement and DISPATCHER will pause or terminate the subscription with no additional charge. DISPATCHER will send out an invoice for any fees due up to the day of the termination.

---

### INDEMNIFICATION

CARRIER shall defend, indemnify, and hold FREIGHT DISPATCHER harmless from and against all loss, liability, damage, delay, claim, fine, cost or expense, including reasonable attorneys' fees, arising out of or in any way related to the performance or breach of this Agreement by CARRIER, its employees or independent contractors working for CARRIER (collectively the "Claims"), including, but not limited to, claims for or related to personal injury (including death), property damage and Claims related to or arising out of CARRIER's possession, use, maintenance, custody or operation of the equipment used for providing transportation services.

---

### TERM AND TERMINATION

This agreement begins on the above date, for a period of one week, and automatically renews each week; except it terminates in the event of any of the following reasons:
- a) FREIGHT DISPATCHER ceases business;
- b) CARRIER ceases business;
- c) Either parties give three (1) day written notice of termination of this agreement.

Any payments sent from CARRIER to FREIGHT DISPATCHER as per this agreement are not refundable.

This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between FREIGHT DISPATCHER and CARRIER and may not be amended, modified, or waived except by written agreement, signed by FREIGHT DISPATCHER and CARRIER. This contract cannot be changed, modified, limited, or supplemented by reference to any CARRIER rates, rules, classification, practice schedule or tariff.

## NOTES FOR ERIN / ONBOARDING AGENT

- This agreement must be signed BEFORE any load is dispatched for a new carrier
- Commission rate field: use 10% for new carriers (first 90 days), 8% for existing
- Payment is due within 3 days of your weekly invoice — track this
- The $100 minimum weekly fee applies even if zero loads were booked
- Florida governing law = where legal disputes go. This does NOT affect Iron Rule #1 (no FL loads)
- "1 day termination notice" — if a carrier tries to quit mid-week, invoice them for current week fees first
- Keep a signed copy on file in Airtable for every active carrier
