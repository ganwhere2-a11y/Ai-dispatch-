## Year 3 Goal: 500 Companies Across All Markets

By Year 3, the system should be handling:
- 500 carrier/fleet companies across USA, Canada, and Europe
- Multiple agent contexts running simultaneously per market
- Shadow Agent fully operational — minimal owner decisions needed
- Command center managing all 3 markets from one dashboard

## Quarterly Check-ins

**Q1 (Months 1-3):** Is the USA operation working? Are Iron Rules holding? Is Erin booking profitable loads?
**Q2 (Months 4-6):** Is revenue growing week-over-week? Is the trial funnel converting? Is Maya reducing owner interruptions?
**Q3 (Months 7-9):** Canada live and profitable? Is the Decision Engine earning autonomous capability on core load types?
**Q4 (Months 10-12):** EU pilot working? Is Shadow Agent making the owner's days easier?

## What Makes the System Unbreakable (Year 2+)

1. Decision Engine has 1,000+ logged decisions — agents act autonomously on 80%+ of loads
2. Shadow Agent has fully profiled owner's judgment — rare situations still get good recommendations
3. All 3 market contexts are fully built with local compliance, carriers, and clients
4. Command Center shows all 3 markets in one dashboard
5. The only things the owner does: approve Tier 3 actions, review weekly reports, and make strategic decisions

## Key Metrics to Track Weekly

- Loads booked per week (target: grows 20% month over month in Year 1)
- Revenue per week
- Quote-to-book conversion rate (target: >40%)
- Iron Rule rejection rate (track by rule — if FL or weight spikes, investigate)
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate (target: >30%)
- Decision Engine autonomous rate (how often do agents act without asking?)
- Client retention rate (target: >90% monthly)
